Design rationale

One system, not three adsEvery format shares the same wordmark, gold rule and cream/ink palette pulled straight from the product photo — recognizable as one campaign at a glance, in any placement.

Motion pace matches attentionVertical social loops fast and bold for a scrolling thumb; feed and banner slow down because both are read, not swiped past.

CTA fits the platform's own vocabularyFilled button on TikTok and the website, since both browsing built for a tap; a text link on Instagram feed, closer to how the platform's own UI reads.
